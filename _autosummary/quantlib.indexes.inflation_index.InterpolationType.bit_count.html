quantlib.indexes.inflation_index.InterpolationType.bit_count¶

InterpolationType.bit_count(/)¶

Number of ones in the binary representation of the absolute value of self.

Also known as the population count.

>>> bin(13)
'0b1101'
>>> (13).bit_count()
3
